Cimple is a platform that helps business brokers collect information about
businesses for sale and share confidential information memorandums ("CIMs")
with prospective buyers.

## 1. Information we collect

**From brokers (our customers):** name, username, email, firm information,
account settings, and the content you create on the platform (deals, notes,
documents).

**From sellers (at the invitation of their broker):** business information
provided through intake forms, document uploads (e.g., financial statements,
tax returns, leases), and conversations with our AI advisor — including the
full conversation transcript and the structured business profile derived from
it.

**From buyers:** name, email, company, investment criteria you choose to
provide (industries, locations, financial capability), decisions you submit
on opportunities, and questions you ask through the Q&A feature.

**Automatically:** engagement analytics inside CIM viewing rooms (pages
viewed, time spent, scroll depth, cursor heat maps) tied to the viewing
buyer's access link; standard server logs (IP address, timestamps); session
cookies required to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising trackers.

## 2. How we use information

- To provide the service: collecting business information, generating CIM
  documents, managing buyer access, and showing brokers engagement analytics.
- To process content with AI: seller interview conversations, uploaded
  documents, and business data are processed by Anthropic's Claude models to
  extract structured information and generate documents. Under Anthropic's
  commercial API terms, this data is not used to train their models.
- To send transactional email (invitations, notifications, reminders,
  password resets) via our email provider.
- To secure the platform (rate limiting, session management, monitoring).

We do not sell personal information. We do not use your data to train AI
models.

## 3. Legal basis and consent (PIPEDA)

We collect, use, and disclose personal information with consent and for
purposes a reasonable person would consider appropriate: sellers provide
information at their broker's invitation for the purpose of marketing their
business; buyers provide information to evaluate opportunities; engagement
analytics inside secure viewing rooms are disclosed to the deal's broker as
part of the service's core function.

## 4. Sharing and sub-processors

We share information only with service providers needed to run the platform:

| Provider | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|
| Railway | Hosting and database | United States |
| Anthropic | AI processing | United States |
| Resend | Email delivery | United States |

Information may be stored and processed outside Canada; where it is, it is
subject to the laws of those jurisdictions. We share information with a
broker's brokerage account as directed by the platform's function (e.g., a
seller's responses are visible to their broker; buyer engagement is visible
to the deal's broker). We may disclose information if required by law.

## 5. Retention

We retain information while the associated brokerage account is active and
for a reasonable period afterward for backup integrity. Brokerages may
request export or deletion of their deal data at any time (see Section 7).

## 6. Security

Security measures include TLS encryption in transit, encryption at rest,
per-brokerage data isolation, hashed passwords, expiring tokenized access
links, server-enforced NDA gating, watermarking, access-controlled document
storage, rate limiting, and continuous monitoring. See our Security Overview
for details. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify affected parties
of a breach as required by law.

## 7. Your rights

Subject to PIPEDA and applicable law, you may request access to, correction
of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting
privacy@cimple.ca. Sellers and buyers may also direct requests through the
broker who invited them. We will respond within the timelines required by
law. You may withdraw consent, subject to contractual and legal limits; note
that withdrawing consent may prevent participation in a transaction process.

## 8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies (session sign-in). No third-party advertising
cookies are set.

## 9. Children

The platform is for business use and not directed to individuals under 18.

## 10. Changes

We will post updates to this policy at app.cimple.ca and update the
effective date. Material changes will be communicated to brokerage account
holders by email.

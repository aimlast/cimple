How it works

From first call to buyer-ready, without the grind.

Four steps. Most of them happen while you're working the next deal.

01

Invite your seller

One secure link. The AI conducts an adaptive interview that probes like a skilled advisor, asks industry-specific questions — bonding for construction, liquor licensing for restaurants, payer mix for clinics — and never asks for anything twice.

02

Every source, one profile

Financial statements, tax returns, leases, call transcripts, public web data — parsed, cross-checked, and merged into a single structured business profile. Discrepancies get flagged, not buried.

03

A CIM that looks designed

The layout engine builds bespoke visual pages: charts, timelines, metric grids. A blind version with a project codename is generated automatically for early-stage buyers.

04

Share, track, decide

Buyers view in secure NDA-gated rooms. You see who read what, for how long — and which buyers actually match the deal.
